## 1. Retail Sector

Modern retail **cannot function** without ML.

### Use Case: Amazon Demand Forecasting

**The Problem:**
- Amazon sells 60+ crore products during sales (Great Indian Festival)
- Cannot stock all products blindly - too expensive, no guarantee of sales
- Need to predict which products will sell

**ML Solution:**
- Data scientists analyze past sales data
- Use **demand forecasting** algorithms to predict what to stock
- **Data mining** reveals purchasing patterns

**Impact:**
- Small prediction errors = crores in losses
- Applies to Flipkart, Myntra, all major e-commerce

---

### Use Case: Big Bazaar Customer Profiling

**Why they ask for your phone number at billing:**

**Data Collection:**
- Track what you buy (milk, health products, spicy food, cosmetics, sports items)

**ML Analysis:**
- Build customer profile based on purchase behavior
- Health products + milk = health-conscious customer
- Spicy food + sports items = different profile

**Business Model:**
- Sell customer profiles to advertisers
- Gyms target health-conscious customers via SMS
- **Targeted marketing >> Random SMS** (higher conversion rates)
- Big Bazaar charges premium for this rich data

**Why this works:**
- You wouldn't give phone number to roadside vendor
- But trust big store ‚Üí they collect valuable data

---

### Use Case: Product Placement

**How stores decide what goes next to what:**

**ML discovers associations:**
- Analyzes co-purchase frequency
- Famous example: Diapers & beer often bought together
- Places related items nearby

**Result:** Increased sales through strategic placement

## 2. Banking & Finance

ML automates decisions and reduces risk.

### Use Case: Loan Approval

**Traditional Process:**
1. Submit profile
2. Wait for human review
3. Get decision

**ML-Enhanced Process:**
1. Submit profile
2. **ML screening:** Compare your profile to database of past defaulters
   - Strong similarity to defaulters ‚Üí High risk ‚Üí Reject
   - Low similarity ‚Üí Pass to human officer
3. Human officer reviews flagged cases

**Other Banking Applications:**
- Optimal branch locations
- Customer-specific promotions
- City-specific financial plans
- Insurance risk assessment
- Share market prediction
- **Algorithmic trading** (buying/selling stocks automatically)

---

## 3. Transportation & Logistics

ML balances supply and demand in real-time.

### Use Case: Ola/Uber Surge Pricing

**Example: Kolkata during office hours or Durga Puja**

**The Problem:**
- High demand (everyone needs ride)
- Low supply (not enough drivers)

**ML Solution:**

**Step 1: Demand Forecasting**
- Predict high-demand areas and times

**Step 2: Dynamic Driver Allocation**
- Notify nearby drivers
- Offer incentives (extra pay) to reach high-demand areas

**Step 3: Surge Pricing**
- Charge users 2x-3x normal price
- Extra money covers driver incentives

**Result:** Supply-demand balanced, drivers earn more, users get rides

---

### Use Case: Route Optimization

**Applications:**
- Google Maps (fastest route)
- Zomato/Swiggy (delivery optimization)
- Logistics companies (truck routes)

**ML determines:**
- Best route considering traffic
- Delivery sequence for multiple orders
- Time estimates

## 4. Manufacturing

High automation requires predictive maintenance.

### Use Case: Tesla Factory

**Setup:**
- Highly automated factory
- Robotic arms assemble entire cars
- Tight production schedules (bookings 6 months in advance)

**The Problem:**
- One robot failure = entire production line stops
- Customer bookings delayed
- Bad reputation, lost sales

**ML Solution: Predictive Maintenance**

**How it works:**

**Step 1: Continuous Monitoring**
- Sensors track temperature, RPM, pressure, vibration
- Data sent to central server in real-time

**Step 2: Anomaly Detection**
- ML identifies patterns before failure
- Example: RPM gradually dropping ‚Üí bearing wearing out

**Step 3: Preventive Action**
- Fix/replace part BEFORE breakdown
- Schedule maintenance during planned downtime

**Benefits:**
- No unexpected delays
- Customer satisfaction maintained
- Prevents cascading failures
